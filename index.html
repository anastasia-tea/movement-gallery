Welcome!

This blog is intended to share my love of art with others!

Every month, I will choose a different art movement from history, and every week, I will highlight a different artist.

In doing so, I hope to introduce my visitors to new ideas and figures that you may not have heard of and to find other people who are excited about art history!

Feel free to subscribe to my newsletter down below where I share new things that I have learned and try to connect with my blog visitors.

I have also included a form where I try to recommend art to you based on the information you share with me there.

Thank you for checking out my blog, and I hope you learn some interesting new things ~
